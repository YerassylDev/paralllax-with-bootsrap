Our Approach


One of the primary things that stand out about Parallax Global is our unique point-of-view and approach to consulting engagements.


The lens through which we view every commlting engagement is a Parallax View, which of course, as the definition of "parallax" suggests, means that we take two nktenate (but not necessanly opposing) views of every problem, and compare and contrast two altenate strategies for approaching the problem. This ensures that individuals, teams and management do not become victims of their oun paradigms (read Thomas Kuhn's The Structure of Scientific Revolutions for more on this), that we dont get lazy, and that we can assure our clients of the deepest and most creative approaches to their problems, every time.


A Parallax Perspective


As we use the term, we mean taking a look at a task, assigumest, problem from two different points of-view. To do this, we assign two diferent teams to engage with an assigument and develop their own points of view, and then present their views to three referees, who then decide, based on the information that we have, which is the better intial path to pursue. But that is only the beginning of the process.

Throughout the engage we contisne to use both points of view as a way of illuminating the "space between them," and achieve greater clarity of observation and elfectiveness of action.


In this way, we protect ourselves from falling victin to reinforcing our own paradigms.


Modeled atter the preat Greek Philosophy, our Parallax view outlines two opposing approaches to the problems we try to collaborate with our chents and resolve. On one hand we use empiricism, viewing what we know from the physical (senses) perspective. We see, we feel, we acquire through being on the ground, taking to people, observing, toaching others creations. On the other hand we use rationalism, thisking, reasosing, hypothesizing and abstracting on the way to determine possible courses of action. We also use ethics, the deep understanding of good and bad, right or mrong, just or not - in short moraiity.


Our views, our processes and our protocols have been honed over and over again, with time, maturing and advancing over time, as we have grown as an orgasization.
